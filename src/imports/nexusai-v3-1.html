FAQ

Questions We
Always Get

Is NexusAI compliant with FINTRAC and OSFI?+

Yes. NexusAI was built PCMLTFA-compliant from the ground up. SAR submissions, audit trails, and risk reporting are natively integrated and designed to satisfy FINTRAC and OSFI examination standards.

How does NexusAI handle the CDBA open banking mandate?+

We are fully Phase 1 compliant with read-only API data sharing and consent management. Our architecture is Phase 2 ready — when write access goes live in 2027, you will not need to rebuild anything.

Does AI replace compliance officers and advisors?+

Never. NexusAI is built on a Human-in-the-Loop (HITL) philosophy. AI handles triage, drafting, and pattern detection — humans make every critical compliance and advisory decision. This is what regulators and Canadians require.

Can government institutions deploy on-premise?+

Yes. Our Government tier includes on-premise deployment, dedicated security audits, and custom compliance workflows designed for federal data sovereignty requirements.

How does NexusAI AML compare to rule-based systems?+

Graph-based AI surfaces complex layering and shell company networks that rule-based systems miss entirely. Our approach reduces false positives by up to 70% while catching sophisticated patterns that evade legacy monitoring.

What makes NexusAI different from other platforms?+

NexusAI is the only platform purpose-built for the Canadian regulatory environment — not adapted from a US or European product. Every feature from SAR generation to CDBA consent management is designed specifically for Canadian institutions.
