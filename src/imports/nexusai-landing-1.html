FAQ

Questions We Always Get

Is NexusAI compliant with FINTRAC and OSFI requirements?+

Yes. NexusAI was built PCMLTFA-compliant from the ground up. SAR submissions, audit trails, and risk reporting are all natively integrated and designed to satisfy FINTRAC and OSFI examination standards.

How does NexusAI handle the CDBA open banking mandate?+

We are fully Phase 1 compliant with read-only API data sharing and consent management. Our architecture is Phase 2 ready — when write access and payment initiation go live in 2027, you will not need to rebuild.

Does AI replace compliance officers and financial advisors?+

Absolutely not. NexusAI is built on a Human-in-the-Loop (HITL) philosophy. AI handles triage, drafting, and pattern detection — humans make every critical compliance and advisory decision. This is the model Canadians trust and regulators require.

Can government institutions deploy NexusAI on-premise?+

Yes. Our Government tier includes on-premise deployment options, dedicated security audits, and custom compliance workflows designed for federal data sovereignty requirements. Contact our government team for a tailored assessment.

How does NexusAI's AML perform compared to rule-based systems?+

Graph-based AI detection surfaces complex layering patterns and shell company networks that traditional rule-based systems miss entirely. Our AI-native approach reduces false positives by up to 70% while catching sophisticated money laundering that evades legacy systems.

What makes NexusAI different from other fintech AI platforms?+

NexusAI is the only platform purpose-built for the Canadian regulatory environment — not adapted from a US or European product. Every feature from SAR generation to CDBA consent management to OSFI reporting is designed specifically for how Canadian institutions operate.
